Dill Salmon

Description

Easy to make, nutritious, and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Butter
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Fresh dill

Steps

  1. Wash and finely chop fresh dill
  2. Combine butter, olive oil, salt, pepper in small pan to melt
  3. Pat dry salmon fillets, apply marinade mixture to salmon
  4. Cook salmon at 375 for 12 minutes
  5. Sprinkle choped dill on salmon filets, cook for 5 more minutes
  6. Remove from oven
